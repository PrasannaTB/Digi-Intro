I was watching a movie called “The Karate Kid” with my sister when I was 8, I guess. I absolutely loved the movie. It’s one of my favourites. In that movie, there was a scene where Dre (the karate kid) was training for Kung Fu which had been shot in The Great Wall of China. I don’t know what fascinated me, but after watching that movie I really wanted to go to The Great Wall of China.

When I told that to my sister, she told me that I could add that to my bucket list. It was then that I heard that term “BUCKET LIST” for the very first time and I planned to make one. Over the years I have been adding all the things I want to do and experience in my life to the list.

Fingers crossed; I hope I get to tick the whole list before I hit the bucket.
