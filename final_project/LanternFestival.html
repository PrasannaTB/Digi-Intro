About Chinese Language

The Chinese language is the oldest written language in the world with around six thousand years of history. Proving that the written Chinese language existed for more than three thousand years, Chinese character inscriptions have been found in turtle shells dating back to Shang dynasty. The Chinese dictionary contains around 40,000 characters. All around the world around 1.2 billion people speak different varieties of Chinese.

The Lantern Festival aka Shangyuan Festival is celebrated on the fifteenth day of the first month of the Lunisolar Chinese calander. This tradition started around 2000 years ago and carries multiple meanings. On the night of the festival, streets are decorated with colorful lanterns with riddles written on them, believed that they bring good fortune. People eat sweet rice balls, watch dragon and lion dances, play games, set off fireworks, etc.
