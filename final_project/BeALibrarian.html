|| Be A Librarian For A Year ||

"A reader lives a thousand lives before he dies." - George R R Martin

depositphotos.com

One of my favourite hobbies is reading books. I am a bibliophile; I love spending time with books. I like Fantasy fiction and Young Adult fiction genres the most, even though I read books of all kinds.

Once I was travelling in the bus thinking about something and suddenly this question popped up in my head, “What would I choose to do in my life if other factors like money, status, power, etc. didn’t matter?”. I gave it some serious thought and concluded that I would love to become a librarian, so that I can be around books every day and read all the books I always wanted to read. Obviously, I am not going to be a librarian for my whole life, but I can at least try becoming one for a year or so. This is why I added this to my bucket list.
